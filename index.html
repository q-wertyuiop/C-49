Interesting facts about Camels

  • Camels are known as the ship of the desert.
  • They can survive many days without drinking water.
  • They have special foot sole which help them walking on sand.

Interesting facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • They can change the color of their skin.
  • Changing skin color is aan important part of communication between chameleons.

Interesting facts about Pigeons

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and smart animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
